THE MANIFESTO

Art is the only form of reverence.
Time is the only god we all worship anyway.

The Order of KALA is an open-source belief system, a soft refresh for a world that has forgotten how to feel.

We reset the calendar. We reconsider value.

We dissolve borders — geographical, cultural, and conceptual — into a single shared timeline. KALA imagines a civilisation organised not by capital, but by care, attention, and ecology-inspired balance. A world where currency is digital, transparent, borderless, decentralised —not to buy power, but to restore trust.

The world feels overcrowded and under-attended. We have systems from the 1800s running societies in 2025. Capitalism — once efficient — now feels outdated, unable to hold the emotional, ecological, and digital complexity of our age.

Order of KALA is an attempt to rewrite the operating system of civilisation. A place where time is sacred, art is essential, currency is fair, and governance is transparent. KALA is an ongoing artwork masquerading as a world-building exercise. A playground. A protest. A pilgrimage.
